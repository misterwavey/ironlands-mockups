Your Action
"Send a small expedition of 4 scouts along the ancient waymarkers… carry enough provisions for 5 days… if the trail leads to an inhabited valley, make peaceful contact — bring copper ingots as a gift…"
What Happened

Your scouts followed the carved waymarkers up through the Grey Pass, tracing symbols that grew older and stranger with each mile. On the third day, the trail descended into a narrow valley thick with ancient oaks — trees untouched by any axe.

They found a small settlement of perhaps forty souls — a reclusive people who call themselves the Ashenborn. They speak an older dialect but could be understood. The copper ingots were received with interest, but the Ashenborn elder grew suspicious when she noticed iron buckles on your scouts' boots. To them, iron is "the cursed metal."

Two scouts were permitted to stay as guests. The other two were sent back with a message: "Bring no iron to this valley, and we may speak further."
