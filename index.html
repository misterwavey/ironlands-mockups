IRONLANDS

A Modern AI-Driven Turn-Based Strategy Game

An open-ended multiplayer 4X game set in a richly populated world. Factions wage war and forge alliances. Cities rise and fall. Economies fluctuate. You lead a wandering tribe through this landscape—exploring, trading, fighting, and navigating the politics of a living world.

The design emphasises roleplaying over resource management. Your tribe is defined by nine numbers (people, food, materials, tools, weapons, trade goods, livestock, mounts, coin). The depth comes from special actions: freeform commands you type in plain English. "Send scouts to negotiate with the Iron Wolves." "Ambush the caravan at dawn." A Game Master interprets your intent and resolves outcomes. One turn per day.
